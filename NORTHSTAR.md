# North Star

Build an AI accountability coach and mentor that helps users achieve short- and long-term goals by continuously monitoring focus, detecting drift, and bringing them back on task through engaging overlay visuals, motivational speech, and other supportive interventions.

Core promise:
- Detect off-task moments quickly and compassionately.
- Deliver clear, actionable guidance that reconnects users to their goals and schedule.
- Improve consistency and streak safety without shame or surveillance creep.

Primary success signal:
- Weekly on-task time and goal completion trend upward while user-reported stress stays flat or improves.
